About Our Project

Empowering farmers with AI-driven plant disease detection

Our Mission

We're dedicated to supporting sustainable agriculture through accessible technology.

Our plant disease detection system helps farmers identify crop diseases early, reducing crop loss and improving yields. By making advanced technology accessible to farmers of all scales, we aim to contribute to food security and sustainable agricultural practices.

How Our System Works

Upload Image

Take a photo of your plant and upload it to our system.

AI Analysis

Our machine learning model analyzes the image to identify diseases and pests.

Get Results

Receive detailed diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

Our Technology

Our disease detection system uses state-of-the-art deep learning techniques:

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for image classification
  • Trained on thousands of plant disease images
  • Detects diseases across multiple crop types
  • Regularly updated with new disease patterns
  • Built with PyTorch and Django

Frequently Asked Questions

Our system achieves an average accuracy of 85-90% across most common crop diseases. The accuracy is continuously improving as we train our models with more data.

Currently, our system can identify diseases in maize, tomato, and onion crops. We're actively working to expand our coverage to include more crop types.

For best results, take clear, well-lit photos that focus on the affected plant parts. Make sure the diseased area is clearly visible and try to capture it from multiple angles if possible. Avoid shadows and blur.
